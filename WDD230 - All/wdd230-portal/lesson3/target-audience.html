Target Audience

Wandering Weather's audience are adult and teenagers who are Florida residents. These people like to spend time outdoors or have to commute to work where they will need to know the weather on a daily basis.

Personas

Nico Robin is a High School student who likes to play volley ball and is on her school team. She drives herself everywhere with the car she got for her birthday.


Edward Elric is a college student who is majoring in Biology. He lives on campus and enjoys hanging out with his friends on the weekends and has two dogs.


Senerios

  • You are planning a camping trip for the next weekend and need to know what the weather will be like so you can make a packing list.
  • You are planning to take some time off work and go to Disneyworld with your family. You need to know which days will be the coolest so you don't get a sunburn.
  • You are home alone and there is a really bad thunderstorm outside. The sky starts turning green and you need to know if a huricane or tornado is coming so you can take shelter in the closet.
  • You need to buy some new summer clothes, but are unsure if it's going to be hot enough to wear them yet.
  • You are new to Florida and unsure what huricane season is going to be like and you want to see if there are any huricanes that could be a threat.
