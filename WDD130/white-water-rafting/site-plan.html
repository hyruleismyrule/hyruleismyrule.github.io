Overview

Purpose

Our Purpose at Epic Water Rafting is to provide quality services to our customers through our field guides, equipment, and customer services. We have many rafting locations across the United States that are Epic. Our motto is “We strive to be the best in the business.” We believe that anyone should be able to water raft without feeling unsafe or unhappy. Our company founder Megan C. Rafter had a passion for white water rafting since she was a child growing up on the river side, and we want to share that passion with the world, and make every experience Epic.

Audience

Our target audience is children and adults between the ages 12-45 to minimize health and safety concerns. Customers should be free from high blood pressure, back, neck, or heart conditions that could be aggravated by this adventure. Our target families are those of the middle to upper financial range. Our customers are looking for a quality water rafting experience, with minimal planning on their part. They are looking for that feeling of thrill and excitement only provided by Epic Water rafting. Most potential customers will be accessing our website through desktop and mobile phones, so they can easily make reservations and change anything if necessary.

The Site Map of a site is just like it sounds…it is a map of the pages in a site and how they are related and linked together. From the map above we can see that we will eventually have the Home page and 2 sub or child pages.

The lines that connect them all together indicate that each page should be accessible from any other page, it is essentially showing us the global navigation for the site.

Wireframes

Wireframes are like blueprints for making webpages. They should show the major sections of content that will be on the page and the relative locations of each element. In the wireframe below you can see there will be 6 sections to our page:

  1. At the top we have a section with the logo (the box with the mountain means an image) and the navigation bar.
  2. Then there is a banner image that stretches all the way across the screen.
  3. Next we have some text and an image
  4. ...followed by another row made up of an image and some text.
  5. Then one more section of text with no image.
  6. Lastly, a footer containing a copyright/name line and 3 social media icons.
