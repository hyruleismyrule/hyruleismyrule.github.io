Overview

Purpose

When was the last time you moved? Was it a challenge to sell your house or find a new one with everything you wanted? Have you ever had a house you loved get taken right out from under your nose? Our website makes it easy to find several homes in the right area and with everything you want… all while staying within your budget! Our site will get you in direct contact with the owner as well as an agent. Our website will also recommend buyers for the house you're selling.

Audience

Our audience are adults who are looking to move. They will be looking for a Real Estate Agent, and a place to list their current home as well as a new home that they will enjoy. Our customers could also be Real Estate agents who are looking for houses to recommend to their clients. Our target demographic covers a wide range of income levels and budgets. These people could be first time home buyers, or looking to expand the houses they rent out. Our site will have everything they are looking for.

Content

Home page

Did you know that the housing market can be used as a stable way to make a lot of money? Because the prices of houses rise and fall slowly over time, it can be used like the stock market by investing when the prices are low and cashing out when the prices are high. With the coaching from our team, you can be prepared to invest at the right time and save up enough for children’s college or future retirement. Or if you would like something more stable, with owning a small property close to you, you can maintain it and rent it out and make money slowly over time until you have enough to buy another property to rent. Sign up for our investor newsletters here! People will come to our site to buy and sell houses quickly. Customers will find everything they need to move all in one place. With our site people will be able to quickly compare houses, their features and their prices to find the best fit for them. Customers will find that our site has a list of capable realtors in many locations that will take them through the process of paperwork and through available houses. Our site also allows the user to put their own house up on the market to get possible buyers interested. Our site will explain the process of buying and selling a house as well as provide realtors who are willing to go over the necessary paperwork.
